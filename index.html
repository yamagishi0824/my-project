About Me

As a licensed medical doctor specializing in diagnostic radiology, I am committed to advancing the integration of machine learning in medical practice. Currently, I am a resident at the Department of Diagnostic Radiology, Toranomon Hospital, and pursuing a PhD at the Graduate School of Medicine, The University of Tokyo. My research focuses on the application of machine learning to medical imaging.

In addition to my academic and clinical work, I actively participate in machine learning competitions, achieving awards in NLP and computer vision (Kaggle Competition Master, with a highest rank of 286th out of approximately 200,000 users). I aim to apply these experiences to advance the application of machine learning in healthcare.
